Salad

In this recipe you will learn to make chopped salad!

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens (e.g., romaine, iceberg, spinach)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup roasted red pepper (from a jar), chopped
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Salad:

    • Wash and dry the mixed greens, then chop them into bite-sized pieces.
    • In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, diced bell pepper, finely chopped red onion, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, chickpeas, and chopped roasted red pepper.

  2. Dress the Salad:

    • Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad.
    • Gently toss the salad ingredients together until they are well coated with the dressing.

  3. Season and Serve:

    • Season the chopped salad with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Divide the salad into individual serving bowls or plates.
    • Enjoy your refreshing Chopped Salad!
