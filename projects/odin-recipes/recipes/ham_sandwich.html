Sandwich

In this recipe, you will learn how to make a ham sandwich!

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 4-6 slices of ham
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cheese (optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Assemble the Sandwich:

    • Place two slices of bread on a clean surface.
    • Spread a layer of mayonnaise on one slice of bread and mustard on the other, or use your favorite condiments.
    • Layer the ham slices on one slice of bread, followed by lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and cheese if desired.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Place the second slice of bread on top, condiment-side down, to create a sandwich.

  2. Cut and Serve:

    • Cut the sandwich in half diagonally or into smaller portions if preferred.
    • Wrap in wax paper or foil for on-the-go enjoyment or serve immediately.
    • Enjoy your classic Ham Sandwich!
