Chicken Teryaki

In this recipe, you will learn how to make chicken teryaki!

The recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  • 1/4 cup sake (Japanese rice wine) or white wine
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Sliced green onions (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Teriyaki Sauce:

    • In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, sake (or white wine), brown sugar, minced garlic, and minced ginger until the sugar is dissolved. This is your teriyaki sauce.

  2. Marinate the Chicken:

    • Place the chicken breasts in a zip-top bag or a shallow dish.
    • Pour half of the teriyaki sauce over the chicken, reserving the other half for later. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate.

  3. Cook the Chicken:

    • Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
    • Remove the chicken from the marinade and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice caramelized glaze from the teriyaki sauce.
    • While cooking, you can baste the chicken with the reserved teriyaki sauce for extra flavor.
    • Remove the chicken from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes.

  4. Serve:

    • Slice the cooked chicken into strips or serve it whole.
    • Drizzle with any remaining teriyaki sauce and garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
    • Serve your delicious Chicken Teriyaki with steamed rice or vegetables.
